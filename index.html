Core Features

Built for minds that work differently

🧩

Adaptive Content Delivery

The AI profiles each learner's style — visual, auditory, reading-based, or kinesthetic — and reformats content accordingly. Dense paragraphs become diagrams. Facts become stories.

⏱️

Sensory-Aware Pacing

Built-in focus timers with Pomodoro-style breaks and calming transitions. Learners with ADHD or sensory sensitivities can set their own rhythm without losing progress.

💬

Plain-Language AI Tutor

Ask any question. The AI explains in simpler words, uses analogies, or rewrites in a preferred tone — direct, friendly, or step-by-step — until it clicks.

📊

Progress That Matters

Visual, non-judgmental progress tracking. No red crosses — just encouragement. Tracks effort, consistency and concept mastery separately to reflect real growth.

🔊

Full Accessibility Stack

Text-to-speech for every piece of content, high-contrast modes, dyslexia-friendly fonts, and keyboard-only navigation baked in from day one.

🌍

South African Context

Designed with the South African curriculum and local language context in mind. Supports code-switching and local examples that resonate with learners on the ground.
